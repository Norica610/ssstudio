Three stages to an eco-city future.

From personal action to collective vision — every step makes a difference. The journey begins at home, not at the power station.

Stage 01 Individual
🌱
Eco-Home
Portable Device Experiment
Start small with portable solar chargers and mini wind turbines for everyday devices. Verify your energy potential at home — no large upfront investment required.
Stage 02 Social
🌿
Eco-Neighbourhood
Community Energy Sharing · 2027
Join local energy cooperatives and share renewable power with your neighbours. From passive customer to active contributor with shared ownership.
Stage 03 Collaborative
🌳
Energy Workshop
Collective Action & Design
Participate in design workshops to imagine and build portable energy generators for different life scenarios. Co-design your city's energy future.

Final Goal: Eco-City Vision

Full grid integration by 2030. A city where data sets democratic strategy, derelict land becomes community energy parks, and every citizen is an active producer — not just a passive consumer paying a bill.

05 — The Journey to 2030

The Journey
to 2030
Eco-City.

"Collab Green-Life" — a digital network that informs people how they can fit in with green energy based on their current situation, budget, and aspirations. Every citizen a potential contributor.

01
Personal Experiment — Now
Use 1–3 portable units to verify your home's energy potential. Grow your first digital seedling. Small-scale start-up begins here.
02
Community Leasing — 2027
Council derelict land becomes available for "People Make Glasgow Communities Renewable." Neighbours co-lease, co-own, and co-generate.
03
Eco-City Integration — 2030
Full grid integration. Citizen-generated data sets democratic energy strategy. Every household active in the energy system.
