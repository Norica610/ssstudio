01. Anatomy

The foundation of the procedure starts with a clear view of your renal arteries—the vessels supplying blood to your kidneys.

02. The Problem

Overactive nerves surrounding these arteries can signal the body to maintain high blood pressure, regardless of diet or exercise.

03. The Procedure

A specialized RDN catheter is inserted to deliver precise energy, calming the overactive nerve signals.

04. The Result

By quieting these nerves, your body naturally returns to a state of equilibrium, leading to sustained blood pressure reduction.
