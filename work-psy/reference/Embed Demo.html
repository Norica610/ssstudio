Colorful Time phone — as iframe

Drop the phone into any page with a single <iframe>. The interactive prototype — 14 screens, the colour test, journal, profile, everything — works exactly as it does standalone.

↑ Live, interactive. Click through splash → onboarding → test → result.

How to drop into your page

Copy these three files into your project, then add the iframe. Tailwind + fonts are loaded by the phone itself.
→ Colorful Time Phone.html
→ colorful-time-phone.css
→ colorful-time-phone.js
→ app-assets/ (all 8 character PNGs)
<iframe
  src="Colorful Time Phone.html"
  width="430" height="880"
  style="border:0;background:transparent"
  title="Colorful Time prototype">
</iframe>
